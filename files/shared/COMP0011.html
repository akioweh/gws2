4. Matrices

A linear map f:V↦Wf : V \mapsto Wf:V↦W where VVV has dimension mmm and WWW nnn can be represented as a m×nm \times nm×n matrix.

Matrix multiplication is the equivalent of function composition, and as such shares all the properties of composition.
